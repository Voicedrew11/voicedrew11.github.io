Day 2 - Tuesday, May 9th 2023

Today went significantly better than yesterday; Many of the problems I experienced on day one have partially subsided. It wasn't all great though.

I've found the first waking period (between 0500 and 0930) to be brutal. I absolutly need to be doing something during this period, or risk falling asleep. Fortunately, this makes the first nap of the day all the more delightful. Speaking of:

Unlike yesterday, I actually got some sleep during both my morning and afternoon nap. Let me tell you, they are positivly wonderful. I wake up feeling like i've slept for hours, all in 20 minutes!

Polyphasic.net strongly recommends against the use of caffine while on a polyphasic sleep schedule. Indeed, that is probably good advice, but not advice I am willing to follow. I can attest that at least at this point in time, coffee has been excelent for getting me through thr 6 hour gap between my monring and afternoon naps. I am worried about caffine affecting the quality of the second nap, but I am also drinking around half of what I normally would. It's probably not a big deal.
